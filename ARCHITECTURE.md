# AgentGuard - AI Agent Marketing Review Platform

## Overview
AgentGuard is an AI-powered platform that reviews marketing content generated by AI agents or human creators. It provides multi-layered validation including PII detection, compliance checking, audience fit analysis, and actionable content improvement suggestions.

## Review Agents (Chained Workflow)

### 1. **PII Detection Agent (AEGIS)**
- **Purpose**: Detect and flag sensitive personal information
- **Detection Patterns**:
  - Email addresses
  - Phone numbers (various formats)
  - Social Security Numbers
  - Credit card numbers
  - IP addresses
  - Physical addresses
- **Output**: List of detected PII with positions, redacted content

### 2. **Compliance Agent (SENTINEL)**
- **Purpose**: Ensure content meets platform and regulatory requirements
- **Checks**:
  - Platform-specific rules (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, TikTok)
  - Prohibited words/phrases
  - Required elements (hashtags, CTA, images)
  - Character limits
  - Regulatory compliance (GDPR, CCPA mentions if needed)
  - Brand guidelines adherence
- **Output**: Compliance score, violations list, risk level

### 3. **Audience Fit Agent (PULSE)**
- **Purpose**: Analyze if content matches target demographic
- **Analysis**:
  - Age appropriateness
  - Regional/cultural sensitivity
  - Interest alignment
  - Tone and language fit
  - Engagement prediction
- **Output**: Fit score, mismatches, demographic recommendations

### 4. **Content Enhancement Agent (CREATIVE)**
- **Purpose**: Provide actionable improvements
- **Suggestions**:
  - Hashtag recommendations (trending, relevant)
  - Optimal posting time (based on platform, region, audience)
  - Image/video recommendations
  - Call-to-action improvements
  - Content structure optimization
  - **Content Rewrites**: Alternative versions with improvements
- **Output**: Structured suggestions, rewritten content variations

## Platform-Specific Rules

### LinkedIn
- Max length: 3000 characters
- Images: Recommended but not required
- Best posting times: Tue-Thu, 9-11 AM local time
- Avoid: Hard sales, emojis overuse, external links without context
- Preferred: Professional tone, thought leadership, data/stats

### Instagram
- Max caption: 2200 characters
- Images/Video: **Required**
- Best posting times: Wed-Fri, 11 AM-1 PM
- Hashtags: 5-10 optimal, 30 max
- Avoid: Text-heavy content without visuals
- Preferred: Visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes, user content

### Twitter/X
- Max length: 280 characters
- Images: Highly recommended
- Best posting times: Mon-Fri, 9 AM-3 PM
- Hashtags: 1-2 optimal
- Avoid: Hashtag spam, controversial topics without strategy
- Preferred: Concise, engaging, timely content

### Facebook
- Max length: 63,206 characters (practical: ~500)
- Best posting times: Wed-Fri, 1-3 PM
- Avoid: Engagement bait, misleading content
- Preferred: Community-building, authentic stories

### TikTok
- Caption: 150 characters max
- Video: **Required**
- Best posting times: Tue-Thu, 7-9 PM
- Hashtags: 3-5 optimal
- Preferred: Authentic, entertaining, trending sounds

Retrieve audit log for a specific review.
